R-emo

RNAemo - RNA research project

Description

This researching calling "Relationship between divergence of using synonymous codons in host-virus and the presence of microRNA" (R-emo) was part of the final work of Franco Riberi. The aim of the study was to determine if this bias could be the result of evolutionary pressure exerted by the miRNA. To achieve this goal massive comparisons should be made (in the order of 10e7) between the recognition of the virus natural genome and the ''humanized'' genome. The latter may be obtained by replacing codons in the viral genome, achieving a codon usage ratio similar to the host. Also allows comparison between secondary structures. This project belongs to FuDePAN.
